About Me

  I'm currently a master student in the BCI&ML Laboratory at Huazhong University of Science and Technology (HUST), advised by Prof. Dongrui Wu. Before that, I obtained my B.S. from the School of Optical and Electronic Information at HUST, advised by Prof. Danhua Cao.

  I have a wide range of interests in Artificial Intelligence, including Brain-Computer Interfaces (BCIs) and Deep Learning. For BCIs, My research focused on the security (like adversarial examples) in BCI systems, such as P300/SSVEP spellers; For Deep Learning, I mainly focuse on some toy problems, including and solutions of different optimization setting.
